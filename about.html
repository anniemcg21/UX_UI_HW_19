About Me

Hi. I am Annie. I am a web designer with DearDoc and reimagine medical professional's websites into beautiful and user friendly experiences for their clients. Inspired by the field of user experience, I want to bring more accessible digital experiences into this world.

My background in event management led me towards this path, and I think of user interactions as guests as an event, it's all about anticapiting their needs and planning their ideal experience. I pride myself in being a meticiulous planner, a thorough researcher, and a considerate teammate.

Born and raised in San Diego, CA and spent fours years on the east coast at Brown University in the beautiful and tiny state of Rhode Island.

Thanks for stopping by. If would like to know more about me or my work, please reach out via email annie.mcgregoruxui@gmail.com
